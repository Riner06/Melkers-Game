About Vocabulary Voyagers

Welcome to Vocabulary Voyagers, a web-based learning game where you embark on an RPG adventure to learn a new language.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make language learning fun and engaging for children and young people. We believe that by incorporating educational content into an exciting RPG adventure, we can make the learning process more enjoyable and effective.

How It Works

When you join Vocabulary Voyagers, you’ll create a profile that allows you to log in and save your progress in the game. You’ll be able to add friends, learn about the game on our information page, and play the game on our dedicated game page. In the game, you’ll have an inventory at the bottom of the screen where you can keep items like swords and other tools that you’ll find on your journey. You’ll also have a map to guide you through the adventure.

Who We Serve

While the game is designed for children and young people, we understand that parents are often the ones investing in educational resources for their children. We’ve made Vocabulary Voyagers with these dedicated parents in mind, offering a valuable learning tool that their children will love to use.

Our Plan

We’re committed to continuous development and improvement of Vocabulary Voyagers. Our prioritized backlog can be found on our GitHub page under Issues. We’re currently working towards our Web E and C milestones and are planning to add an API for enhanced functionality.

Testing

We’re dedicated to ensuring a smooth and enjoyable user experience. While the primary use of our site is intended for computers, we’re also optimizing for various screen dimensions to accommodate mobiles and tablets. However, please note that the game may not be playable on devices without a keyboard.

Join us on this exciting journey and let’s learn together!
